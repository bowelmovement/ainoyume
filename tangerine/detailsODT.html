One Dandy Tulip pays a tribute to the main character of Pretear, Himeno Awayuki. The shrine is about 75% done, and aiNET's only shrine to the main character from a series.

t h e   h i s t o r y ;

*scratches head* One Dandy Tulip's debut was a breeze, surprisingly. The main layout didn't match my usual style, and recently, I've begun to hate it. ^^; It took me forever to come up with a decent name for this shrine. Then, I was reminded of a scene in the show one day, matched a few terms up and voila! Not much history on this one, it was pretty much all thumbs up from the beginning. In the future though, I plan to convert the coding into PHP.

t h e   o p i n i o n a t e d ;

In many ways, Himeno reminds me of Tsukino Usagi from Sailormoon. She's always late for school, clumsy, funny, and sort of ditzy. She has a best friend that's a bookworm and she loves to eat sweets. Yet, there are many ways Himeno differs from Usagi. She has a black belt in Tai Kwan Do and she enjoys strange flavored ice cream. --; She's quite a charismatic character, and I love that about her.
