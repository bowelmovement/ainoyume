To this day, Reflection remains the net's only shrine dedicated to the Mirror card from CardCaptor Sakura. In fact, there are virtually no shrines on the web dedicated to the clow cards at all. Reflection is also the second oldest site on aiNET, dating back to mid-2001. At the moment, it is possibly the least active site.

t h e   h i s t o r y ;

Reflection was created shortly after TVD was finished. At the beginning, as with TVD, I had intended to make a general CardCaptor Sakura site, called "CardCaptor Sakura Fantasy Paradise," or CCSFP. Now that I look back on it, the name was simply too amusing. After only 2 versions, I scrapped the idea and had a brainstorm. After some consideration, I decided to create a clow card shrine. A shrine to The Watery card was my original intention, but that idea was scrapped as well. Finally, the Mirror card was chosen. I had the hardest time coming up with a good design for the site, and went through numerous trial layouts (a clow card layout can be a lot harder than you think. x_x). The site was launched after about 2 months of work. I produced as much information as I possibly could out of the few episodes the Mirror appeared in. Nowadays, I seem to only update with wallpapers. ^^;

What prompted me to make a shrine to a clow card? That may be what you're wondering. Frankly, I adore the clow cards. At a young age, I was easily fascinated by the spiritual world, and seeing the Clow Cards in action were sometimes my favorite part of the show. I liked the way they were drawn, how they were presented, and the mysterious aura they seemed to give off every time they were called. It was pretty hard choosing a favorite, but the Mirror was just so sweet and gorgeous in its own way. So, although Reflection is now partially static, it's still one of my favorite shrines.
