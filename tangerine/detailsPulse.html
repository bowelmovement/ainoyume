PULSE is a general message board, shared between Tas, and Jay, and I, hosted @ CloverpetalsCOM. If you have a habit of being insane or just like to chat and meet new friends, the PULSE forums may be an ideal place for you.

PULSE was created after the 3 of us decided that a message board would be fun to have. We couldn't figure out who's domain to have it on at first, since we all had some domain issues at the time. So, I offered to host it and Jay set it up. It was originally named Doki-Doki, the sound of the heartbeat in Japanese. It was later changed to PULSE (seeing a trend? XD;) because it sounded better. ^^; Later, PULSE was moved to Tas's domain and switched to be powered by phpBS instead of Ikonboard.
