Originally, I didn't think I would be listed at thefanlistings.org for the Gravitation fanlisting. By the time I wanted to make the Gravitation fanlisting, it was already in the "upcoming" section. The rules required the upcoming fanlistings to be made within a month. So, I waited patiently for about half a year. When I checked back 6 months later, it was still in the upcoming section, so I emailed the staff member who was in charge of the anime/manga section of the fanlistings and asked for permission to take it over. About a month later, I received the OK and set to work. Credit actually goes to Di for coming up with the title of the site.
